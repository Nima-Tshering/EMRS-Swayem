State Level Cultural & Literary Fest 2023

August 26, 2023

The SWD of Government of Sikkim organised the State Level Cultural & Literary Fest 2023, where students and teachers from 4 EMRS (Eklavya Model Residential Schools) participated in various events held at Janta Bhawan, Gangtok today. Programme was graced by Special Secretary, Social Welfare Department, Shri. Suman Gurung as the chief guest, Principals, faculties, students of EMRS, officials and staff of Social Welfare Department, Government of Sikkim. The competition, was a platform for the students of EMRS to showcase their creative talent and to hone their skills for participating in national levels.

The event consisted of Elocution competition, Storytelling competition, Creative Writing competition, Painting competition, Extempore competition, Debate competition, Spell Bee competition, Recitation competition and Quiz competition. The programme had the participation of students from four EMRS Schools located in Swayam, Mangan District, Gangyap, Gyalshing District, Suntoley, Namchi District and Parkha, Pakyong District. EMRS Swayem garnered 8 gold and 6 silver medals in various categories displaying their utmost discipline,hardwork and endurance.
